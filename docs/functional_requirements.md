### **Roles & Functionalities**
#### **1. Admin**
- Manage all futsal grounds, owners, and users.
- Approve or reject futsal ground registrations.
- Generate platform-wide reports (e.g., revenue, bookings, user activity).
- View and manage all transactions.
- Handle disputes and customer support.

#### **2. Futsal Ground Owner**
- Register as an owner and create a futsal company profile.
- Add multiple futsal grounds with details (e.g., name, location, size, surface type).
- Upload ground photos.
- Manage available time slots for booking.
- View and manage bookings.
- Generate reports for revenue, bookings, and customer data.
- Receive payments for bookings.

#### **3. User**
- Register and log in.
- Search and filter futsal grounds by location, availability, and features.
- View ground details and photos.
- Book available time slots.
- Make online payments for bookings.
- View booking history.
- Cancel or reschedule bookings (based on owner’s policy).
- Rate and review futsal grounds.

### **Key Features**
- **Multi-tenancy:** Each owner manages their own futsal grounds independently.
- **Real-time Booking System:** Prevents double bookings.
- **Payment Integration:** Supports online transactions.
- **Reporting & Analytics:** Provides insights into bookings, revenue, and customer activity.
- **Notifications:** Email/SMS notifications for bookings, payments, and cancellations.
- **User Reviews & Ratings:** Helps users choose better futsal grounds.
- **Admin Dashboard:** Centralized management and analytics.
